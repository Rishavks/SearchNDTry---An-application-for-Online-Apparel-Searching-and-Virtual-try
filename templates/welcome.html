About

Find Out More About Us

SearchNDTry application is developed to facilitate online apparel searching, virtual try and visiting the respective site for online purchase

SearchNDTry project has a Web as well as Mobile Application.

  • The App will facilitate users to search apparels using an image and virtually try before purchasing.

Uploading images, image based online searching, and the extraction of apparel from selected images are among the features available. Other capabilities such as managing a user's cart per registered account, virtual try-ons and different website features have also been developed.
